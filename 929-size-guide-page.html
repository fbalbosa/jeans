Size Guide

Find your perfect 929 fit.

Because each pair of 929 Jeans is made to order, choosing the right size and fit is important.

Use this guide to measure your body, compare your measurements, and decide whether 929 High or 929 Low is right for you.

01

Measure Your Waist

Measure where you want your jeans to sit: natural waist for 929 High or below the hip for 929 Low.

02

Measure Your Hips

Wrap the tape around the fullest part of your hips. Keep the tape level and relaxed.

03

Choose Your Length

Select Petite, Regular, or Tall depending on your height and how you want the jeans to fall over your boots.

How to measure.

Use a soft measuring tape and stand naturally. Do not pull the tape too tight. Suede has structure, so we recommend choosing the size that best matches your actual measurements.

  • Natural WaistMeasure the smallest part of your waist.
  • Low WaistMeasure where a low-rise jean would sit, usually below the navel.
  • HipsMeasure the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • InseamMeasure from the inner thigh down to where you want the hem to land.
