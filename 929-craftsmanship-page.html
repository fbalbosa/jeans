Brazil is renowned for its mastery of leather. Our partners are family-owned ateliers with decades of experience working with some of the world’s most luxurious materials. Each pair of 929 Jeans is cut, sewn, and finished in Brazil with pride, precision, and care.

The Making of 929 Jeans

1. Selecting the Finest

We source only the softest lambskin suede, chosen for its supple hand, durability, and natural beauty.

2. Precisely Cut

Skilled hands cut each piece with precision to ensure the perfect fit and clean lines.

3. Expertly Sewn

Our artisans bring each piece to life with expert stitching and reinforced construction.

4. Refined Details

Custom hardware, tonal stitching, and thoughtful finishing touches elevate every pair.

5. Garment Finished

Each pair is washed and brushed to enhance the suede’s buttery softness and rich texture.

6. Made for You

Inspected with care, wrapped beautifully, and shipped directly to you — made to order.
